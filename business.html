Our advantages

Our training is given face-to-face at the Multihexa Saguenay premises. However, if you are elsewhere in Quebec, you have the possibility to follow our online training courses. Additionally, we can also organize training sessions at your premises.

We offer a needs assessment at no cost or obligation. Whether you are a beginner, intermediate or advanced, we will personalize our services according to your needs, in order to provide you with an optimal learning experience.

The duration of the training is flexible and can be adjusted in blocks of 3, 4 or 6 hours, depending on your preferences. Training dates are set based on your availability and that of the trainer.

During the training, you will have the necessary equipment at your disposal and you will be supervised by experienced trainers who will support you throughout your courses.
