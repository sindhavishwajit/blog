Study in Vancouver

Welcome

One of Multihexa College campuses in British Columbia sits in Vancouver, a bustling west coast seaport and the major urban centre of western Canada. As of 2022, the city is also ranked as the fifth most livable city in the world by the U.K.-based Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU).

Vancouver is one of Canada's largest tech hubs. The rapidly evolving tech industry in British Columbia employs 100,000 tech professionals, around 75,000 of whom work in Metro Vancouver.

Multihexa College campus location in the heart of this metropolitan area is strategic and beneficial to its students. By being surrounded by IT giants and startups, the college has a better understanding of the needs of the industry, allowing it to tailor its web development programs and graphic design, and digital marketing certificates to include the most in-demand skills in tech.

Reasons to study in Quebec

British Columbia

Vancouver offers the best of both the city and natural wonders. Students can go hiking in the mountains, take a swim at the beach, or bike around the city all on the same day. When winter comes, they can head to the city's famous ski resorts. With Vancouver's transit buses and SkyTrain, getting around the city is a breeze. Students can use these highly efficient commuting systems to visit the many tourist spots Vancouver has to offer, such as the Capilano Suspension Bridge Park or the cable cars up at Grouse Mountain.

Things to know before arriving in Vancouver

Quality of life

Recognized for its exceptional quality of life, Vancouver is a safe province that will provide you with enriching experiences and offer you several opportunities. You will benefit from its low cost of living and accessibility to the health system.

Quality of education

The province of Vancouver is renowned for its higher education institutions. The education offering is varied and of high quality. Our motto: innovation at the next level.

Cultural diversity

British Columbia is a multicultural city. You will have the opportunity to learn Quebec traditions and interact with individuals from different origins.

The Weather in Vancouver

The question everyone is asking: is it cold in Vancouver?

The weather in Vancouver is unique since all four seasons are present. You will be able to observe and feel several climates in a single year.

In autumn, the leaves on the trees change colors before falling to the ground. In winter, the landscape transforms. It becomes all white and snowy. In spring, the fresh air slowly warms up and life begins again. In summer, the mercury heats up and can reach around 30 degrees Celsius on the hottest days.

Student Services

Welcome

Before leaving your country, we will have online meetings with you, to check that your trip is well organized.

When you arrive in Canada, our team will greet you to welcome you and help you settle in comfortably.

  • When you land in Montreal, your transportation will be planned to take you to your accommodation or to British Columbia.
  • When you arrive in British Columbia, a member of the team will be there to accompany you to your accommodation.
  • A welcome week is planned before the start of classes. Students finalise their registration, discover the city during organised visits and various activities. Finally, informative sessions planned to introduce you to the essential things for living in Quebec such as medical insurance, bank account, housing, public transportation, etc.

Student life

Your experience at Multithexa will undoubtedly exceed your expectations and allow you to see everything that the province of Quebec can offer you. Our team of teachers will guide you throughout your training and prepare you for the job market.

During your course, you will have access to the following services:

  • Employment assistance: preparation of curriculum vitae (CV).
  • Conferences on the world of IT.
  • Student life: activities to discover Vancouver.
  • Placement service for the end-of-course internship.
  • Help with job search at the end of training.
