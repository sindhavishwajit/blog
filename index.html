Our campuses

ONLINE EVERYWHERE IN VANCOUVER

All our training is available online, live. You will attend classes at the same time as students in class to benefit from the group dynamic. The same support and availability of teachers are offered to you. In addition, activities are planned throughout the training year. Multihexa offers you a next-level experience, no matter where you are in Vancouver.

LOCATION A

This campus offers you a very interesting job market. It is possible to follow your training in class, online or in hybrid mode. Multihexa is directly located in a building which brings together more than 800 people working in the field of technology. You will therefore be close to employers of choice and a bustling digital district.

LOCATION B

Located in the heart of downtown Montreal, this campus, open since September 2023, offers students a dynamic and accessible environment. It is possible to follow your training in class, online or in hybrid mode. On site, you will find a cafeteria and state-of-the-art computer equipment, in addition to training support.
