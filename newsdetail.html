Access to a financial assistance program

We understand that studying privately can represent a significant financial investment. This is why various study financial aid programs are available to make your journey more accessible. Our dedicated team will support you every step of the way, from applying for student financial aid to finding scholarships and grants. In addition, for students from remote regions of Quebec, such as Saguenay, up to 86% of tuition fees can be refunded as a tax credit, thus facilitating your transition to a higher quality education.

Length of training : 1 year

We understand the importance of combining theory and practice in the ever-changing field of information technology. That's why our program is designed to offer you the perfect balance between a solid theoretical foundation and practical experience. With a total training duration of one year and three months, including an internship period, you will be ready to take on the challenges of the professional world and take innovation to the next level at your work!

The different learning formats

Multihexa offers three learning modes: online (synchronous), in class or hybrid mode

The flexibility of virtual with the synchronous online option allows you to interact in real time with qualified instructors and other students, no matter where you are. For total immersion, our classroom sessions offer an enriching student life experience. And if you're looking for the perfect balance, our hybrid mode intelligently combines the benefits of in-person and online teaching, giving you the opportunity to successfully achieve your goals.
