About

Innovation at the next level

Welcome to Multihexa! Since 1986, we have been training true information technology (IT) specialists. We have chosen to concentrate our activities in this area only and this is what allows us to offer information technology programs and, above all, superior quality education. For nearly 40 years, our private college has been the leader in training for the field of information technology.

Many benefits come with training at Multihexa!

THE SHORT DURATION OF THE TRAINING

You can follow your training for one year full-time (5 days/week) or for 18 to 21 months part-time (2 to 3 days/week)

COLLEGE SPECIALIZATION

We've been operating as a training leader in information technology since 1986. It's what allows you to learn specialized skills and take innovation to the next level.

EVERYWHERE IN VANCOUVER

Take your training online, in class or in hybrid mode.

BRITISH COLUMBIA CAMPUS

One of Multihexa College campuses in British Columbia sits in Vancouver, a bustling west coast seaport and the major urban centre of western Canada. As of 2022, the city is also ranked as the fifth most livable city in the world by the U.K.-based Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU).

VERY FAVORABLE ADMISSION CONDITIONS

In order to provide quality education to all, here are the minimum requirements we ask :

  • Secondary 5 or equivalent
  • No computer knowledge required
  • No advanced math course required

ACCESS TO A FINANCIAL AID PROGRAM FOR STUDIES

Multihexa is an educational institution recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education. You can benefit from the financial assistance program for studies from the Government of Vancouver.

ACCESS TO VARIOUS TAX CREDITS

By registering with Multihexa, you could be eligible for certain tax credits. Depending on your geographic location, tax credits can pay for up to 86% of your training.
